The Healing Home Run for New York and the Country

The Mets played the Atlanta Braves on Sept 21, 2001
It was the first baseball game to be played post the incident of 9/11.

The country was traumatized but still strong.
Firefighters, NYPD, supporters all came together in unity.

The Mets were trailing by 2-1 and on the eight innings, IT HAPPENED
Mike Piazza scored a 2 run homer taking the Mets to a score of 3-2.

Note: That game brought the whole stadium and the whole of NY together. Its said that started the healing process for NY, a moment lived only a few times
